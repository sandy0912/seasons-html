Why Visit Udupi? Best Places, Beaches & Experiences in Karnataka’s Coastal Gem

Planning a vacation in Karnataka? Discover Udupi, a must-visit coastal city known for its stunning beaches, vibrant temples, and delicious vegetarian cuisine. Whether you’re searching for tourist places in Udupi or top things to do in Udupi, this travel guide has you covered for 2025.

“Golden sands, spiritual sites, and authentic South Indian flavors — Udupi welcomes every traveler.”

Sri Krishna Temple Udupi is one of Karnataka’s most famous pilgrimage destinations. Witness traditional pujas, intricate architecture, and join in lively festivals that attract thousands of visitors annually.

Love the sea? Don’t miss Malpe Beach and Kaup Beach, popular for their clean shores, water sports, and breathtaking sunsets. Take a short ferry ride to St. Mary’s Island, one of India’s unique geological wonders, featuring rare basalt rock formations.

Immerse yourself in Udupi’s culture and food. The town is famous for its pure vegetarian restaurants (“Udupi hotels”), classical Yakshagana dance, and vibrant local festivals. For nature lovers, the area also offers river backwaters and green countryside near the Arabian Sea.

Must-Do in Udupi

Udupi delights visitors with its perfect mix of coastal and cultural experiences. For a memorable trip, don’t miss out on these essentials.

  • Visit Beaches and Temples.

  • Try Kayaking & Adventure Sports

  • Taste Seafood & Local Cuisine

From sunrise on the sand to flavorful dinners and spiritual moments, Udupi is made for unique travel memories.
